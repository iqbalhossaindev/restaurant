About us

A modern Dubai restaurant built around warm hospitality, premium ingredients, and beautifully plated comfort food.

Kestford Restaurant is based in Dubai, UAE and serves a curated menu of signature bowls, pasta, noodles, premium rice dishes, and fresh comfort plates. Our kitchen blends international flavours with a polished dining style so every dish feels vibrant, generous, and memorable.

Our story

Kestford was created as a restaurant for guests who want elevated comfort food in a calm, elegant setting. We focus on rich flavour, balanced presentation, and service that feels smooth from the first welcome to the final bite.

From signature biryani bowls to modern noodle plates and chef-led favourites, our menu is designed to feel familiar and premium at the same time. Every dish on the menu is priced in AED and prepared for guests who enjoy quality, consistency, and a polished dining experience.
